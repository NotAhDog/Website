About this site

This website is the official portfolio of Edward, showcasing a body of work that spans digital art,
photography, printmaking, and elements of sculpture and painting. Edward’s creative practice is
rooted in minimalism, often merging realistic and abstract approaches to explore themes that
reflect his inner world and personal identity.

Each artwork presented here is part of a broader exploration — a visual language shaped by emotion,
experience, and self-reflection. From experimental prints to quiet photographic studies and dynamic
digital pieces, the portfolio captures a multidimensional artistic voice.

In addition to a curated selection of top artworks, the site highlights influential artist models who
have shaped Edward’s vision and process. It’s a space to not only view the work, but to understand
the thinking and inspiration behind it.


About Me

My name is Edward Mathers, and I’m a multidisciplinary artist with a deep passion for visual
expression and the endless possibilities of design. Art has always been a powerful outlet for me — a
way to translate thoughts, emotions, and identity into something tangible and lasting.

My work is primarily rooted in a minimalistic style, often blending clean, simple forms with touches
of realism. I enjoy exploring contrasts — the quietness of negative space paired with intricate details,
or the balance between abstract forms and realistic representation. Each piece I create is a personal
exploration, shaped by my experiences and evolving creative voice.

I'm constantly striving to improve and refine my skills through regular practice, experimentation, and
learning from the artists who inspire me. My long-term goal is to become a tattoo artist, a field that
has fascinated me for years. I’ve always been drawn to the powerful, personal nature of tattoos — how
they carry stories, symbolism, and artistry on the canvas of the body.

This journey is ongoing, and this portfolio is both a reflection of where I am now and a stepping stone
toward where I hope to go.
