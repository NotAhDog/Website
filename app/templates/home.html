Basic Introduction

Welcome to the portfolio of Edward, a multidisciplinary artist exploring identity
through digital art, photography, printmaking, and occasional sculpture and painting.
Blending minimalism with both abstract and realistic styles, this space is a
reflection of personal expression, creative curiosity, and visual storytelling.


Who am I?

I’m Edward, and I have a deep appreciation for the diversity of artistic subjects and
the unique designs that can emerge through creative exploration. My favorite areas of
focus are Design, Printmaking, and Photography — each offering its own way to
experiment with form, texture, and visual storytelling. I’m passionate about discovering
how different mediums can be used to express ideas in fresh and personal ways.
